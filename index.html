Hungry, Hungry Something

Find fun recipes at random or using ingredients in your fridge!

Little application to find fun new recipes! In the below search box you can look up recipes at random or enter a specific ingredient you'd like to use.

See something you like? Feel free to save it! We'll make you a grocery list for everything you'd like to make!

Change your mind on something? You can remove it! Be careful, once you've deleted something, you won't be able to bring it back.
